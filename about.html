About MyOnlineMeal

Delivering happiness one meal at a time since 2020. We're passionate about connecting food lovers with the best restaurants in their city.

📖 Our Journey

How It All Started

MyOnlineMeal was born from a simple idea: everyone deserves access to delicious, high-quality food, delivered fast and fresh. Founded in 2020 during challenging times, we saw an opportunity to bridge the gap between amazing restaurants and hungry customers.

What started as a small team of food enthusiasts has grown into India's most trusted food delivery platform, serving millions of customers across multiple cities. Our commitment to quality, speed, and customer satisfaction remains at the heart of everything we do.
