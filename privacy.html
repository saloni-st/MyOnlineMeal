Privacy Policy

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, date of birth
  • Profile Information: Profile picture, preferences, dietary restrictions
  • Delivery Information: Delivery addresses, special instructions
  • Payment Information: Credit/debit card details, payment preferences (processed securely through our payment partners)

Order and Usage Information

  • Order history and preferences
  • Restaurant ratings and reviews
  • Customer service interactions
  • App usage patterns and preferences

Location Information

  • Delivery addresses
  • Device location (with your permission) for delivery tracking
  • IP address for service optimization

2. How We Use Your Information

Service Provision

  • Process and fulfill your food orders
  • Provide customer support and resolve issues
  • Send order confirmations and delivery updates
  • Facilitate payments and prevent fraud

Service Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve our app and website
  • Personalize your experience and recommendations
  • Develop new features and services
  • Conduct research and analytics

Communication

  • Send promotional offers and updates (with your consent)
  • Notify you about account changes or policy updates
  • Provide customer service support

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

Service Partners

  • Restaurants: Order details and delivery preferences
  • Delivery Partners: Delivery address and contact information
  • Payment Processors: Payment information for transaction processing
  • Technology Partners: Analytics and app functionality providers

Legal Requirements

  • Compliance with applicable laws and regulations
  • Response to legal requests from authorities
  • Protection of our rights and safety
  • Prevention of fraud and abuse

Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of the business transaction.

4. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Encryption: All data transmission is encrypted using industry-standard SSL/TLS protocols
  • Secure Storage: Personal information is stored on secure servers with restricted access
  • Access Controls: Limited access to personal information on a need-to-know basis
  • Regular Audits: Regular security assessments and penetration testing
  • Employee Training: All employees receive data protection and security training

While we implement these measures, no system is completely secure. We encourage you to protect your account credentials and report any suspicious activity.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us improve your experience on our platform.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic website functionality
  • Performance Cookies: Help us analyze website usage and performance
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings
  • Marketing Cookies: Used to deliver relevant advertisements (with your consent)

Managing Cookies

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. Note that disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality.

6. Your Rights and Choices

Account Management

  • Access: View and download your personal information
  • Update: Correct or update your account information
  • Delete: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Portability: Export your data in a portable format

Communication Preferences

  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Manage notification settings
  • Control email and SMS preferences

Location Settings

  • Enable or disable location services
  • Manage location data collection

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@myonlinemeal.com or through your account settings.

7. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. We will:

  • Notify you of significant changes via email or app notification
  • Post the updated policy on our website with the revision date
  • Provide a summary of key changes when applicable

Your continued use of our services after policy updates constitutes acceptance of the revised terms.
