❓ Quick Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

How can I track my order?

+

You can track your order in real-time through our app or website. Just go to 'My Orders' and click on your current order to see live tracking.

What is your refund policy?

+

We offer full refunds for orders that are significantly delayed, incorrect, or if there are quality issues. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days.

How do I become a restaurant partner?

+

Visit our restaurant partner page or contact us at partners@myonlinemeal.com. Our team will guide you through the onboarding process.

What are your delivery charges?

+

Standard delivery charge is ₹29 per order. However, delivery is free for orders above ₹299. Premium members enjoy free delivery on all orders.

How can I cancel my order?

+

You can cancel your order within 2 minutes of placing it through the app or website. After that, please contact our support team for assistance.

Do you offer corporate catering?

+

Yes! We provide comprehensive corporate catering solutions including daily meals, meeting catering, and event services. Contact us for a custom quote.
